MISSION BRIEFING

You are the last line of defense. Pilot your ship across a hostile alien world, destroy the invading forces, and protect the human colonists.

The world wraps horizontally. Watch your radar. Every human saved is a victory.

THE ENEMIES
LANDER
+155 PTS
  • • Slow-moving green ships that patrol the surface
  • • Primary threat: They grab humans and try to carry them off the top of the screen
  • • Both landers and baiters fire projectiles at your ship
  • • Shoot a lander while it's carrying a human to drop the hostage
  • • If a lander escapes upward with a human, that colonist is lost permanently
BAITER
+230 PTS
  • • Fast, aggressive red interceptors
  • • Appear once your score exceeds ~3500 and become more common on higher waves
  • • Home in on your ship and fire plasma bolts
  • • Extremely dangerous in groups — use your shield and smart bombs wisely
PROTECT THE HUMANS

There are 10 humans on the surface at the start of each game. They are marked in cyan on the radar.

WHEN A LANDER GRABS ONE
The human is lifted upward. Shoot the lander to release them — they will fall toward the ground.
RESCUE (850 PTS)
Position your ship to catch a falling human before they hit the ground. A successful rescue flashes "HUMAN RESCUED" and awards massive points.
Humans that hit the terrain are lost. Landers that reach the top of the screen with a captive permanently remove that human from the world.
YOUR ARSENAL
LASER
RAPID FIRE
Your primary weapon. Fires in the direction your ship is facing. Excellent for destroying landers (especially ones carrying humans) so you can rescue their captives.
SHIELD
3 SEC BURST • 6 SEC COOLDOWN
Temporary energy bubble that blocks all damage from enemies and enemy fire (both contact and projectiles). Lets you safely fly through swarms or tough spots. Press (or hold) SHIFT or right-click to activate.
B
SMART BOMB
START WITH 3
Detonates a massive blast centered on your ship. Destroys every enemy within a wide radius and drops any humans they were carrying (no rescue points awarded). Earn more bombs by reaching score milestones and advancing waves.

SURVIVAL TIPS
  • ▸ Always watch the radar — landers can be far away grabbing people.
  • ▸ Prioritize landers carrying humans over everything else.
  • ▸ Rescue points (850) are the fastest way to earn extra lives and bombs.
  • ▸ Save smart bombs for dense groups or when you're overwhelmed by baiters.
  • ▸ Slow down near the ground. Crashing at high speed is fatal even with hull remaining.
  • ▸ Use the shield aggressively when baiters are swarming — it has a short cooldown.
  • ▸ The world is seamless. Enemies and humans loop around the edges.
  • ▸ After dying you get a brief period of invulnerability — use it to reposition.
